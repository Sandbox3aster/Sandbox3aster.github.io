Brief Biography

I am a research assistant at Visual Computing Lab, Shangaitech University. My advisor is Prof. Shenghua Gao. Prior to that, I received my my B. Eng degree from Tongji University, Shanghai in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Shangzhi Xu.

My research interest includes Computer Vision and Machine Learning, in particular task such as Saliency detection and Segmentation.
